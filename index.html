About Me

Hello, My name is Michael Sanford. Welcome to my page.

Here's the part where I'm supposed to impress the heck out of you by talking about all my satisfied clients or all my great accomplishments. Instead, I would like to take this opportunity to share with you some of my personal values and what you can expect when you work with me.

People come first: “Our prime purpose in this life is to help others. And if you can't help them, at least don't hurt them.” ― Dalai Lama XIV
At the end of the day if my client is not happy than I'm not happy. This is why I put 110% into every job I do. It all starts with strong communication right from the beginning. How can I meet your requirements if I don't know exactly what they are? This means starting by asking all the right questions and making sure we are both on the same page right from day 1.

Honesty: “Conscience is a man's compass." - Vincent Van Gogh
If I could sum up how I live my life in 5 words these would be them. The most important thing to me is making sure I do the right thing. If for some reason I'm not the right person for the job I'm going to let you know. I know my strengths and weaknesses very well and I would much rather make sure your needs are taken care of than take a job that I can't over-deliver on.

Never stop learning: “Wonder is the beginning of wisdom.” - Socrates
One of my true joys in life is learning. I truly believe that a day that goes by that I don't learn something is wasted day. I may not have all the answers but I know how to find them and I know just how awesome it is to solve a problem I've never encountered.
